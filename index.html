VIRTUAL WORKSHOP


Navigating through the unforeseen pandemic, humankind has learned to cope and hope with better resilience than ever. Adapting to the change and perpetuating the yearly tradition of the Institution, the 9th edition of TechFest is hosted online. This time, with grandiose and poise.

ABOUT

TechFest is one of the South India's largest Techno Cultural Event conducted by TechFest Technology. Inter College competitions, sessions, exhibitions,Y-Talks,Pro-shows,Sports and much more will be part of the TechFest. This year TechFest has been scheduled from 21th May to 26th May. The objective of TechFest is to showcase the dynamism of young Indians today and to inspire them to aspire for a greater India. And this year we are working on Women Empowerment as our social campaign theme.
